    YOUR MAC SELECTIONS

    • The Homeroom Classic Mac

      The original! Our extra-cheesy remake of the cheddar mac you ate as a kid.

    • Gilroy Garlic Mac

      Our best-selling mac! Creamy gouda, salty Italian pecorino, and just the right amount of garlic.

    • Mexican Mac

      Niman Ranch chorizo, jack cheese, and homemade chipotle adobo, served with cilantro and a lime wedge. Can be made vegetarian with soyrizo!

    • Smoky Bacon Mac

      A rich, bold mix of smoked cheddar and jack cheese—with lots of bacon!

    • Sriracha Mac

      So addicting! Spicy sriracha, mixed with a touch of ginger, scallions, and melty havarti.

    • Hawaiian Mac

      A Homeroom team favorite! Salty, sweet, and creamy with bacon, havarti cheese, scallions, and pineapple.

    • Buffalo Chicken Mac

      Tangy, spicy, and topped with our homemade ranch! Grilled chicken, cheddar and jack cheese, scallions, Frank’s Red Hot buffalo sauce, and breadcrumbs.

    • Mac the Goat

      A lighter, elegant mac. Fresh Sonoma goat cheese, jack cheese, scallions, breadcrumbs, and a drizzle of olive oil.

    • Jalapeño Popper Mac

      Our most popular spicy mac, with sharp cheddar, cream cheese, hot peppers, and crispy breadcrumbs.

    • White Cheddar Mac

      Our sharpest mac, featuring a tangy 7-year-aged white cheddar.

    • Macximus

      For the veggie lovers: spinach dip gets a cheesy makeover! Spinach, artichoke hearts, shallots, feta, and jack cheese. 

    • Vegan Mac

      We make a rich and creamy vegan mac sauce out of tofu, a touch of soy sauce, and our secret spice blend. Dairy-free, but sure to please even the biggest cheese fanatics! 

  • Spicy Cauliflower

    Roasted with paprika, pecorino cheese, and a bit of crushed red pepper.

  • Roasted Brussels Sprouts

    Tossed with bacon in a tasty apple cider vinaigrette. (Available vegan!)

  • Minty Buttery Peas

    With a touch of salt, garlic butter, and a sprinkle of fresh mint.

  • Broccoli+Ranch

    Served chilled—make it vegan with our balsamic vinaigrette instead of ranch!

  • Buttermilk Biscuit

    Comes with a side of whipped honey butter.

  • Mixed Greens Salad

    Tossed with one of our homemade dressings: balsamic, citrus, or ranch. Perfect for sharing; make it your own with a couple of add-ins! Or, get a small side salad.

  • The Homeroom Oreo

    Giant and chewy, topped with a sprinkle of sea salt.

  • Big Fudgy Brownie

    Big and super fudgy, with chunks of chocolate.

  • Creamy Peanut Butter Pie

    Indulgent peanut butter filling inside a crunchy, buttery graham cracker crust.

  • Grasshopper Pie

    Mint chip ice cream in a dark chocolate cookie crust, drizzled with homemade chocolate sauce.

  • Banana Creme Pie

    Layers of homemade vanilla custard and caramel with fresh bananas and vanilla wafers—served in a lil’ mason jar!

  • Vegan Gluten-Free Brownie

    From our friends at the Inspired Cookie Co./City Baking.

  • Beer and Wine

    At the restaurant, we have a great selection of local beers on tap—as well as in bottles and cans!—along with a great variety of California wines. Alternatively, you can take your favorite bottles+cans from Homeroom To-Go to enjoy from the comfort of your own home.

  • Homemade Limeade

    Made fresh daily with lime juice and cane sugar.

  • Paradise Iced Tea

    Tropical, fruity, black tea; unsweetened. It's caffeinated, too!

  • Arnold Palmer

    Half house limeade+half paradise iced tea.

  • Iced Coffee

    With Jittery John’s Cold Brew and milk—specify whole or soy. A staff favorite!

  • Sodas

    Mexican Coke; Boylan's Diet Cola, Root Beer, and Ginger Ale; 7-Up; and Pellegrino.
